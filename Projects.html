Flower Vase

For this project, I used the program CMU to make an image out of different shapes. I decided to make a vase filled with flowers, because I like flowers and I thought they would be fun to make with CMUâ€™s shapes since they tend to be symmetrical. I used lines to make the stems, polygons to make the leaves, and the flowers themselves are stars and ovals and circles. I made the vase by combining ovals and rectangles, and colored them with gradients to give the illusion of dimension. The window in the background is made of rectangles and lines for details. In the end, I put a bee beside it because I thought it was cute.

April 26 2024

Polaroid Camera

This assignment was to make a game in CMU using mouse presses and releases. I made a camera that takes different colored polaroids based on where the mouse is pressed. The camera is a collection of shapes in different shades of pink. The movement in the camera is to make the lens larger and then smaller, to make a flash appear, and have the polaroid start printing. Then when the mouse is released, the polaroid goes to its full size and reveals its color, the flash disappears, and the lens goes back to its normal color. Click one more time to reset the camera and take the photo away. Finally, if statements used the x and y values to decide what color to make the polaroid.
